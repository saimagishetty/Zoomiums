FAQ

What does inventory management software do? +
  • System which keeps a track of Stock Inventory in the warehouse
  • Receiving items into a warehouse or other locations
  • Picking, packing and shipping items from a warehouse.
What are some of the advantages of using inventory management software? +
  • Helps in increased efficiency by auto collecting data, conduct Calculations and create records
  • Helps companies cut expenses by minimizing the amount of unwanted parts and products in storage
  • Helps companies keep lost sales to a minimum by having enough stock on hand to meet demand.
What is a healthy inventory level? +
  • Healthy Inventory is holding enough stock while keeping stale and excess stock levels low.
  • Leaving Stocks unaccounted or unattended might cause business losses in the longer run.
  • Level of exact count with one point access with information to all precisely.
What is the maximum inventory level? +
  • The maximum stock level of a period is large enough to cover the demands in the following periods within the maximum days' supply.
  • The maximum stock level is one of the ways in which businesses control the number of goods in their warehouses.
  • The maximum level of stock is usually achieved when those goods arrive which were ordered at the re-order level of the stock.
